## Indicative Offer(s) Principles

**Parties:**
- Nippon Sanso
- LBC Tank Terminals

| Section | Terminalling Key Principles | Cracking Key Principles |
|---|---|---|
| **Scope of the indicative offer** | Only applies to the terminal services described below | Only applies to the Cracking Services described. Indicative fees and terms will be further developed into a first binding Heads of Agreement. |
| **Services covered** | • Unload refrigerated Ammonia from vessels and throughput to re-export exit points<br>• Load pressurized and (semi-) refrigerated NH3 onto barges, coasters or midsize vessels and load pressurized NH3 onto trains | The ACU will receive NH3 from the connected LBC Ammonia Terminal and cracker will deliver H2 into the Hynetwork H2-Backbone. |
| **Fee & capacity commitment** | 40 (+/- 40%) EUR/T NH3 Throughput fee; 100% Top<br>Yr 1 - Not less than 300 Ktpa; by 2035, not less than 700 Ktpa. Additional capacity at discounted price. | Monthly Cracking Service Fee (FMSV) of 1/12 of 50,000,000 € — i.e. **4,167,000 € per month** for a Full Capacity Reservation of 100 KTPA. |
| **Key conditions** | 15 years commitment with extension option (T&Cs to be negotiated). Title remains with customer. Operator has custody of the product. Break fee to apply for "binding capacity reservation" until firm contract executed. Early termination incl. "no fault" termination. NH3 Specifications. Allowance for maintenance period. Physically Commingled product; administrative segregation available. | Minimum 15 years commitment with option to extend. Title & risk of the products (NH3 and H2) remain with customer. Operator has custody. Uninterrupted Feedstock supply considered. Break fee to apply for "binding capacity reservation" until final Ammonia Cracking Service Agreement (ACSA) is signed, taking several scenarios into account (incl. FID not achieved, no proceedings, no fault termination, ...). NH3-feedstock and H2-off take Specifications (Purity, Flows, Pressure and Temperature) to apply. |
| **Others — General legal provisions** | applicable law = NL, customer Credit support, public announcement | applicable law = NL; Parent guarantee; Scheduled and unscheduled maintenance period; Delivery points and title and risk; Availability and Reliability; Yield; Liability clause. |
